---
title: Why plain files still win
date: 2026-04-27
tags: [writing, systems]
---

For a small blog, plain files solve most of the right problems:

- they are easy to back up
- they are easy to diff
- they are easy to move between tools
- they are still readable without the original software

That is the design center for this project. The content should stay useful even
if the generator changes later.
